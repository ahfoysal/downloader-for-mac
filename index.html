Install the browser extension

Click your browser to open its Extensions page and Finder with the unpacked extension ready to drag-drop.
Steps after clicking:
1. Your browser opens at its Extensions page
2. Finder shows the chrome/ or firefox/ folder — drag it onto the Extensions page
3. Enable Developer mode first (Chrome) or click "Load Temporary Add-on" (Firefox)
4. Done — the banner up top flips to "✓ Connected"
